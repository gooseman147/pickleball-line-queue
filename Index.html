Our Mission

Pickleball Line Queue was created to solve a growing problem in the world of public pickleball: long wait times, unorganized court rotations, and unfair matchups between players of vastly different skill levels.

We aim to reduce frustration and increase fun by offering an easy-to-use system. At participating courts, players simply scan a posted QR code to check in. No app needed.

Once checked in, players enter their skill level. Our smart queueing system then organizes players based on arrival time and skill, matching people with similarly skilled opponents whenever possible.

This way, everyone gets more play time, better matches, and a smoother experience — all without the chaos of paddle stacks or shouting over the net.
